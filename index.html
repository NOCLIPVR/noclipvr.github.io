Noclip VR

A Multiplayer Horror Experience

Noclip VR is a multiplayer horror game with unique locomotion and proximity chat. Explore terrifying levels, evade mysterious entities, and solve challenging puzzles with your friends.

"If you're not careful and noclip out of reality in the wrong areas, you'll end up in the Backrooms…"

Endless yellow rooms. Buzzing fluorescent lights. And something lurking in the dark that’s heard you.
