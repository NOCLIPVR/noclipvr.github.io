Level Fun

Find keys and unlock matching doors to get fuses.
Avoid 3 roaming partygoers. Use fuses to repair the elevator.

Level 3: "Electrical Station"

Find buttons to unlock areas, get a keycard, and activate the next area.

Level 5: "Terror Hotel" & Boiler Room

Fix rotated paintings to open a safe, find the green key, lighter, and dynamite.
Use them to blast open blocked stairs. Then turn valves and use a keycard.

Level 4: "Abandoned Office"

Find 3 digits and use the keypad to escape. Try all combinations (6/9 mixups).

Level 8: "Cave Systems"

Explore until you find the lit exit elevator. All players must reach it.

Escape Guide

Objective:
Escape using an elevator fixed by finding 3 fuses.

Mechanics:
- 6 players required
- Partygoers roam and capture players
- Jailed players must be freed by teammates

Keys/Fuses:
- Keys match fuse door colors
- Multiple stories to explore
- Jail = game over if everyone is caught

Strategy:
- Hide under tables
- Rescue teammates
- Place fuses and escape
